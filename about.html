A bit about me.

My name is Justin Baumann, and I am not your typical web developer. I have been a Football Coach/Business Teacher for the last 7 years. I decided to pursue opportunities that allows me to spend more time with family and allows some creative freedom.

Throughout my time as a teacher and coach, I developed a passion for computers and technology. I was always considered "The Tech Guy," on every football staff and teaching department I was in. I taught Web Design for a number of years which allowed me to gain some inside knowledge on building websites. So naturally, I believe website develop is a perfect evolution for my work journey.

I have a loving wife and a beautiful daughter that motivate me to be the best at what I do.
